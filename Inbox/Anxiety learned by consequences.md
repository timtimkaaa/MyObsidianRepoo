This type of learning is also known as "operant conditioning".

Put plainly, if we repeatedly experience a pleasant or desirable consequence after we behave in a certain way, we’re more likely to repeat that behavior. If we experience an unpleasant or painful consequence after behaving in a certain way, we’re less likely to behave that way again
