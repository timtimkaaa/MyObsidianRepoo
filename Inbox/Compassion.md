Compassion involves more than just kindness. 

>[!note] The Dalai Lama defines
> **Compassion** - a sensitivity to the suffering of others, with a commitment to do something about it. 
> 
> He points to two key elements: 
> - attention (sensitivity) 
> - motivation (commitment).
