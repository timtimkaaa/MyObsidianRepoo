# Jazz Progressions
#jazz #theory 

ii V I

## Major

Cmaj: Dmin7 G7 Cmaj7

>Bassline: 
>II + 4th = V
>V -5th = I

You can also use ii-V or V-I

## Minor 
Actually it's harmonic minor, so you're welcome : [[Scales#harmonic minor]]

Cmin: Dø G7 Cm△ 

Sometimes Cm△ is too intense, so you should use Cm7:

Cmin:

>Dø    G7  ---->  Cm△ (intense)
>or
>Dø    G7  ---->  Cm7  (casual)

Dø = Dmin7♭5 - [[Major_har#Seventh]]
Cm△ = C-△ = C minor-major7th
